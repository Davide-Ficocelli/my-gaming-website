About the game

God of War III is an action-adventure game developed by Santa Monica Studio and published by Sony Computer Entertainment. Released in 2010 for PlayStation 3, it concludes Kratos' revenge story against the Olympian gods. The game features brutal combat with chained blades, epic boss battles against Greek deities, and cinematic set pieces that push the PS3 hardware to its limits.

Visit the official playstation website

Key Features:

  • Visceral combat with multiple weapons
  • Epic scale boss battles
  • Quick time event executions
  • Puzzle-solving elements
  • Mythological Greek setting

Why I like it

I remember being hyped for this game while I was playing the previous installments, I couldn't wait to get to God of War III. To this day I can say the hype hasn't been disappointed. What I've been loving the most about this game is the music and sound effects, especially the track "Rage of sparta" which always gets me pumped up. The story was quite engaging, and although good graphics aren't usually an important aspect to me when it comes to judging a game, I have to say that God of War III really stood out to me having played the previous ps2 games which were of course not as graphically impressive and I didn't mind that.
